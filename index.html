Good day and welcome

What is this website about?

On this website I summarise my publically available work. That is, GitHub repositories, planned projects and development statuses of existing ones, and scientific work in theoretical physics.

What are my interests?

I am interested in theoretical atomic physics, (analog) quantum simulation of molecular processes with Rydberg atoms and ions, and (classical) computer programming in general, but particularly in the languages C and FORTRAN. I am mostly interested in simple (see below) open-source computer programs, offering full control to the user.

Philosophy (regarding computer code)

In my personal projects I focus on simple programs, which do not come with loads of ‘unnecessary’ functionality and offer full control to the user by following a more ‘hands-on’ philosophy than programs typically do today — meaing that I typically rather sacrifice unser-friendliness for control and performance, and the possibility to omit the inclusion of third-party software in my projects.
